HEALTHY KITCHEN

Tip-1

Call it minimal-waste or zero-waste, it’s a term that is redefining today’s world. It’s the easiest and actionable thing that we can do in order to become environment-friendly.

Tip-2

Whenever possible, opt for local produce from the farmer’s market. You might be wondering, how this would help, right? Well, they will not provide the fresh produce wrapped or packaged and you can directly keep them in your own bag.

Tip-3

Plastic bags are a complete NO-NO in today’s time. Whenever you have to go out for grocery shopping, take your reusable bags with you. Also, you can keep reusable jars and boxes when it comes to food storage in your kitchen
